Welcome to Mil-an National Highschool

Formerly known as Baguio City National Highschool(BCNHS) - Loakan Annex but signed as an independent school by former President Gloria Macapagal - Arroyo on December 31, 2009. Mil-an National Highschool is one of the most known schools in Loakan. During the pandemic, the school started to make adjustments in order to still teach even though a pandemic is happening.

K-12

K-12 (also K to 12) is an education system under the Department of Education that aims to enhance the learners' basic skills, and produce more competent citizens, and prepare graduates for a a lifelong learning and employment. The 'K' stands for Kindergarten while '12' refers to the number of years of the students basic education (Starts with the 6 years of elementary and another 6 years in Highschool.)
